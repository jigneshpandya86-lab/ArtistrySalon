Our Story

About Artistray

More than a salon.
A sanctuary.

Artistray Salon was born out of a passion for beauty, art, and the transformative power of self-care. Located in the heart of Vadodara at Lotus Elite, Gotri Road, we are a premium beauty studio dedicated to every woman who walks through our doors.

Our name says it all — we are artists, and every client is our canvas. Whether it's a sleek haircut, a bridal transformation, a rejuvenating facial, or a perfectly shaped brow — we approach every service with craft, care, and attention to detail.

We believe that beauty isn't about perfection — it's about confidence. Our team is trained to listen, to understand your needs, and to bring out the best version of you.
